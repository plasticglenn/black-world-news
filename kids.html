Meet Someone Special

Brave people who show us how to stand tall.

MG

Marcus Garvey

🇯🇲 Jamaica

A man from Jamaica who told every Black child to stand tall and be proud.

Marcus Garvey was born in Jamaica. He travelled the world telling Black people one big thing: be proud of who you are. He taught that your skin, your hair, and your story are beautiful. He started the Black Star Line so the Black world could stand together. The black star on our logo comes from him. He would tell you: you are somebody.

  • He was born in Jamaica in 1887.
  • He told millions of people to be proud to be Black.
  • The black star on our logo is his idea.

“Up, you mighty people. You can do what you will.”

TL

Toussaint Louverture

🇭🇹 Haiti

A brave leader who showed that you are never too small to do something big.

Toussaint Louverture lived in Haiti long ago. People told him he could not be free. He did not believe them. He was clever, he was brave, and he never gave up. He led his people until Haiti became the first country in the world where free Black people ruled themselves. He shows us that being brave can change everything.

  • He was born in Haiti around 1743.
  • He taught himself to read and became a great leader.
  • He helped make Haiti the first free Black country.

“You can cut the tree, but the roots of freedom will grow again.”

YA

Yaa Asantewaa

🇬🇭 Ghana

A queen in Ghana who was brave and stood up tall for her people.

Yaa Asantewaa was a queen mother of the Asante people in Ghana. When her people were afraid, she stood up and was brave for everyone. She showed that girls can be strong leaders too. She helped protect the Golden Stool, the heart of her kingdom. She teaches us to be brave even when others are scared.

  • She was a queen mother of the Asante people in Ghana.
  • She was a brave leader when her people needed her most.
  • She shows that girls can be strong and lead.

“If the men will not go forward, then we women will.”

A Place to Know

Wonderful places around the Black world.

🇯🇲

🇯🇲 Jamaica

An island in the warm Caribbean Sea where reggae music was born.

Jamaica is a beautiful island in the warm Caribbean Sea. It is the home of reggae music and the famous singer Bob Marley. People there enjoy the sunshine, the sea, and tasty food like rice and peas.

Did you know? Reggae music was born in Jamaica.

A word from here: Wah gwaan means hello.

🇭🇹

🇭🇹 Haiti

The first country where free Black people ruled themselves.

Haiti is a country in the Caribbean Sea. Long ago, the people of Haiti won their freedom and made the first country in the world ruled by free Black people. People there speak Haitian Creole and French, and they love music and art.

Did you know? Haiti was the first free Black republic in the world.

A word from here: Sak pasé means what is happening.

🇬🇭

🇬🇭 Ghana

A country in West Africa famous for gold and colourful cloth.

Ghana is a country in West Africa. It is famous for shiny gold, colourful kente cloth, and the black star on its flag. Children there play a fun game with seeds called oware.

Did you know? The black star on Ghana's flag stands for African freedom.

A word from here: Akwaaba means welcome.
