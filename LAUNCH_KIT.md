## 📅 First week of posts (suggested cadence)

Start with one post a day. Mix:
- **Story shares** — generated by `social_post.py` from the archive
- **Featured story announcements** — when you change the homepage feature
- **Trends notes** — point at the Trends page when something interesting shows up

Do not post more than three times a day in week one. Build trust before frequency.

## ⚠️ Things to avoid in week one

- Don't link back to old posts of the previous account
- Don't engage with old followers privately about the rebrand — let them discover it
- Don't reply to anyone who screenshots/recalls the old account — neutral silence is the right tone
- Don't post about the agent, the tech, or the project itself yet — just publish stories

The work speaks. The vibe is wire service. Let people find the analysis underneath quietly.
